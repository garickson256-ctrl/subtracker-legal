Privacy Policy

SubTracker · Last updated: May 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SubTracker (the "App") handles your information. SubTracker is provided by the developer of SubTracker ("we", "us", or "our") and is based in Ukraine. By using SubTracker, you agree to the terms of this Policy.

1. Information We Collect

SubTracker is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers or to any third party.

All data you enter into the App — including subscription names, prices, billing dates, categories, currencies, notification preferences, and theme settings — is stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this data.

2. Permissions Used

SubTracker requests the following Android permissions:

  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS) — used solely to display local reminders before your subscriptions renew. Notifications are generated on your device and are not sent through any external server.

We do not request access to your contacts, location, camera, microphone, SMS messages, call logs, or any other sensitive data.

3. Third-Party Services

This version of SubTracker does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or external SDKs that collect user data.

4. Children's Privacy

SubTracker is a general-purpose utility application and is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children, as we do not collect personal information from anyone.

5. Data Security

Because all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on the security of your device itself. We recommend using a screen lock and keeping your operating system up to date. Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all subscription data stored by SubTracker.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained on your device for as long as SubTracker is installed. You may delete individual subscriptions within the App at any time, or remove all SubTracker data by uninstalling the App or clearing the App's storage through your device's system settings.

7. International Users

SubTracker can be used from any country. Because no personal data leaves your device, no cross-border data transfers occur.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the Policy was last revised. Significant changes will be reflected in App updates published through the Google Play Store.
